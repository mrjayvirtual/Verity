What does Verity actually check? Tap to see ▾
Verity flags writing patterns like
  • Absolute words ("always," "100%")
  • Numbers with no source attached
  • Vague backing ("studies show...")
  • Overconfident certainty
  • Cause-and-effect claims with no proof
It cannot tell you
  • Whether something is actually true
  • Real-world facts, people, or events
  • Anything needing outside knowledge
